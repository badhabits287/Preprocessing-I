Background.

Born and raised in Montevideo, Uruguay. have always been a curious one, taking toys apart and reading whatever i could get a hold of. After migrating to the United States, i have explored many different career paths from Antique restoration, Book illustrator, Urban Sketching , Professional tattoo artist and currently boiler mechanic, where i fell in love with the mechanics and basic programming of machinery.
